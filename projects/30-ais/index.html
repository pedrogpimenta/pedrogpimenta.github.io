AIS Channel

App made as a better front-end for the McDonaldsCupons.cat website. The website was losing users and it was clear: the website needed a better way for users to interact with.

The users were able to register, login with the previous account, get and redeem coupons in a much easier way, just showing their smartphone in the restaurant with the open coupon.

We also built a notification when you were near a McDonald's to actively engage with the user.
